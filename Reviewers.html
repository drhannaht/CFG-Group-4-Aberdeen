Meet Our Reviewers

This website is run by a small team of four with a shared love of reading, and for sharing book reviews with the world!

Rebecca

Rebecca is an English Literature and Film/Television Studies graduate from the University of Glasgow. When not reviewing books for a coding project, her favourite pastime is forcing her friends to watch tv programmes so she can discuss it with them at length later. Rebecca enjoys musicals, socialising with her friends, and trying (and failing) to get better at running. Rebecca has no current plans to go into web development, but has really enjoyed challenging herself by learning a skill that is outside of her comfort zone, and looks forward to impressing people with it in job interviews.

Muna

Muna is a Business and Management graduate from Aston University. A lover of numbers, she is currently pursing her accounting qualification.

When not playing around with numbers, she is reading a book. Her love for books first developed when her English lesson studied Noughts & Crosses by Malorie Blackman and she has been hooked ever since. Aside from reading books, she loves hanging out with friends, travelling and binge watching tv shows. Muna's current favourite book is The Great Gatsby

Chloe

Chloe is a Chinese studies student from Hull university. When she graduates she will do a Masters in China and make up for the year abroad which was tragically cut short due to the pandemic. When she isn't binge-watching dramas online, she loves to read non-fiction, satire, and dystopian books though she does have a soft spot for short and sweet stories. Her other hobbies include listening to music, watching musicals, and travelling.

Hannah

Hannah is a Product Manager of MedTech products. Hannah is a scientist by training, she completed a PhD on the link between type 2 diabetes and large bowel cancer. She loves to do crafts, keep fit and read in her spare time. She reads a mix of self-improvememt books, autobiographies, fact and fiction.
